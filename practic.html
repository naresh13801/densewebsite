"E-cell or entrepreneurship cell of Nit Silchar is a non-profit organization run by the students established with the aim of promoting and nurturing entrepreneurship and start-ups within the college. In a world where everyone is a company themselves by the virtue of their ideas, we thrive to develop the attitude of self-dependency among the students. Since its inception, E Cell has grown exponentially, paving the way for the confluence of ideas and imagination by successfully implementing several projects to assist young entrepreneurs."
"Srijan 2.0, the national innovation and Entrepreneurship summit 2021 was a momentous event, with high-profile industry experts, panel discussions, specialised workshops, and case study analysis among the centerpieces. Various events, such as Boardroom meetings, IPL Auction, and Business Quizz, encouraged the spirit of hustling and instilled the zest for start-up ideas among the participants!"
